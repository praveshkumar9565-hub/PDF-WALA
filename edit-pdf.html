About Edit PDF Online Tool

PDFwala.in Edit PDF Tool allows users to add text overlays to PDF documents directly inside their browser. This secure, client-side editor enables you to upload a PDF file, preview it instantly, and add custom text anywhere on the page. You can adjust text size, color, and position before downloading the edited PDF.

All editing processes are performed locally within your browser. No files are uploaded to any external server, ensuring complete privacy and data protection.

How to Use the Edit PDF Tool

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Wait for the preview to load.
  3. Enter the text you want to add.
  4. Choose color and font size.
  5. Drag the text to your desired position.
  6. Click Download Edited PDF after progress completes.
