What Does Flatten PDF Mean?

Flattening a PDF converts all interactive elements such as form fields, annotations, checkboxes, digital signatures, and layers into static content. After flattening, the document becomes non-editable and secure for sharing.

Why Should You Flatten a PDF?

Flattening ensures that recipients cannot modify form inputs or remove annotations. It preserves layout integrity and prevents accidental changes to important documents.
