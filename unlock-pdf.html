About this Tool

Unlock PDF tool allows you to remove password protection from PDF documents if you know the correct password. All processing happens locally in your browser.

How to Use

1. Upload password protected PDF.
2. Enter correct password.
3. Click Unlock.
4. Download unlocked PDF.

Privacy Policy

Your PDF files are processed locally in your browser. We do not upload, store, or access your documents.

Terms & Conditions
